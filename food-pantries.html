Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about food pantry assistance

Who can get food from a food pantry?

Anyone in need. Food pantries serve people regardless of religion, background, or immigration status. Most locations do not require proof of income.

How often can I visit a food pantry?

Frequency varies by location — some allow weekly visits, others monthly. Contact your local pantry directly for their specific policy.

What do I need to bring?

Most locations just need a name and address. Some may ask for ID or proof of residency. Call ahead to confirm what your local pantry requires.

How do I donate food?

Bring non-perishable items directly to any food pantry location, or contact your local center to arrange a food drive or large donation.
