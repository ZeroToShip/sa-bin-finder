About Adult Rehabilitation Centers

💼

Work Therapy

Participants work in thrift stores and donation centers, developing job skills and building structure and routine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the rehabilitation program

How much does the program cost?

The Adult Rehabilitation Program is completely free to participants. Housing, meals, counseling, and all program costs are covered.

How long is the program?

The standard program is 6 months of residential treatment, including work therapy, counseling, and life skills development.

Who is eligible for the program?

Adult men and women struggling with alcohol or drug dependency. Participants must be willing to commit to the full program and follow community guidelines.

How do I apply or refer someone?

Contact your nearest Adult Rehabilitation Center directly by phone. Walk-ins are welcome at many locations, or call to schedule an intake interview.

Is this program faith-based?

Yes, it includes a spiritual component, but the program is open to people of all faiths and backgrounds. You do not need to be religious to participate.

What if there's no center near me?

Call 1-800-SAL-ARMY (1-800-725-2769) for guidance on the nearest available program or alternative support services.

Need Immediate Help?

If you or someone you know is in crisis, please reach out right away.

📞 Call: 1-800-725-2769 📞 Crisis Line: 988
